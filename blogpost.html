Cord Bows

A simple coquette style decoration, perfect for a personal touch.

Materials: 3ply yarn (choose your favourite colour!) and 1.25mm hook

  1. Start by chaining 3.
  2. Insert hook into 2nd ch from hook, yarn over and draw up a loop, insert hook into next ch, yarn over and draw up a loop (3 loops on hook).
  3. Drop 2 loops from hook.
  4. Hold onto the 2 loops with your hand.
  5. Yarn over and draw through remaining loop on hook.
  6. Insert hook into first droppep loop, yarn over and draw through (2 loops on hook).
  7. Insert hook into second dropped loop, yarn over and draw through (3 loops on hook).
  8. Repeat this for desired length of i-cord.
  9. Cut yarn, leaving a long yarn end.
  10. Draw end through all 3 loop on hook.
  11. Pull tight to fasten off.
  12. Big eye needle to hide inside.
  13. Use needle to wrap the thread.
  14. Secure and cut the remaining thread.

Done!
